# AI Engineering Class (Foundation)

Language Understanding Lab, Myanmar

## Course Overview

The AI Engineering (Foundation) course is an introductory graduate-level class covering the fundamentals of machine learning and artificial intelligence. It is designed for students who are interested in pursuing cutting-edge research in this field. The course introduces the overall pipeline of AI and NLP research and development, along with several hands-on practical experiments.

## Instructor

Dr. Ye Kyaw Thu (Lab Leader), together with senior lab members.
